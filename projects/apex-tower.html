Apex Tower

45,000 SF Class A Office Development

Project Overview

Apex Tower stands as a testament to modern commercial excellence in Houston's thriving Galleria district. This 45,000 square foot Class A office development redefines what businesses expect from their workspace.

The building's striking glass façade maximizes natural light while its LEED Gold certification demonstrates our commitment to sustainable construction. Floor-to-ceiling windows offer panoramic views of the Houston skyline.

Premium amenities include a state-of-the-art fitness center, executive conference facilities, a rooftop terrace, and ground-floor retail spaces that create a vibrant mixed-use environment.
