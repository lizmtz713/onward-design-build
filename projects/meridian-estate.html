RESIDENTIAL • RIVER OAKS

The Meridian Estate

12,000 SF of Timeless Elegance

Project Overview

Nestled in the heart of River Oaks, Houston's most prestigious neighborhood, The Meridian Estate represents the pinnacle of luxury residential construction. This 12,000 square foot masterpiece seamlessly blends contemporary architecture with timeless elegance.

Every element of this residence was meticulously crafted to exceed expectations—from the grand double-height foyer with its custom chandelier to the chef's kitchen featuring La Cornue appliances and book-matched marble countertops.

The outdoor living spaces rival the interior in sophistication, with an infinity-edge pool, outdoor kitchen, and manicured gardens designed by a renowned landscape architect.
