The Harrington Residence

Historic Preservation Meets Modern Luxury

Project Overview

The Harrington Residence presented a unique challenge: honor the architectural heritage of a 1920s estate while transforming it into a modern family home. Located in Houston's culturally rich Museum District, this project demanded both reverence and vision.

Our team meticulously restored original features—hand-carved moldings, leaded glass windows, and hardwood floors—while seamlessly integrating contemporary amenities. The original servant quarters became a chef's kitchen with hidden appliances and custom cabinetry.

The result is a home that tells two stories: one of Houston's elegant past and another of sophisticated modern living. Every room celebrates this dialogue between eras.
