Montrose Retail Center

15,000 SF of Vibrant Retail Space

Project Overview

The Montrose Retail Center brings sophisticated commercial space to one of Houston's most creative and eclectic neighborhoods. This 15,000 square foot development was designed to complement Montrose's unique character while providing premium retail environments.

The building features flexible floor plates that accommodate everything from boutique retailers to restaurants and galleries. Exposed brick, industrial steel, and abundant natural light create spaces that feel authentic to the neighborhood.

Wide sidewalks, outdoor seating areas, and public art installations create a welcoming streetscape that encourages foot traffic and community gathering—essential elements for retail success in today's market.
