Tanglewood Modern

8,200 SF Contemporary Masterpiece

Project Overview

Tanglewood Modern represents the future of luxury living in Houston. This 8,200 square foot contemporary residence pushes boundaries with its bold geometric forms, walls of glass, and seamless indoor-outdoor living spaces.

The home was designed for a family who values both minimalist aesthetics and maximum functionality. Open floor plans flow effortlessly from one space to another, while smart home technology provides invisible convenience throughout.

Sustainable features include solar panels, a geothermal HVAC system, and rainwater harvesting—proving that luxury and environmental responsibility can coexist beautifully.
