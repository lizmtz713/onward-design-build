West University Revival

Historic Home Modernization

Project Overview

West University Revival demonstrates how a beloved family home can be transformed for modern living without sacrificing its historic character. This 1950s Tudor-style residence in West University Place received a comprehensive modernization that doubled its living space.

The original home's charming exterior was preserved—steep pitched roof, decorative half-timbering, and arched entryways intact—while the interior was completely reimagined. A seamless two-story addition added 3,000 square feet of new living space.

The heart of the renovation is a stunning open-concept kitchen and family room that connects to a new covered outdoor living area. Modern amenities meet classic craftsmanship throughout.
