OUR STORY

A decade of
excellence in Houston

Onward Design Build was founded with a singular vision: to elevate Houston's architectural landscape through unwavering commitment to quality, innovation, and partnership with our clients.

What began as a passion for exceptional craftsmanship has grown into one of Houston's most respected design-build firms. Today, we're trusted by discerning homeowners, investors, and developers who understand that true luxury lies in the details.

Every project we undertake is an opportunity to create something remarkable—a space that will stand for generations, a legacy built on excellence.

THE VISIONARY

Meet
Shahbazz Anwar

With over 15 years of experience in construction and development, Shahbazz Anwar has established himself as one of Houston's most trusted names in luxury building.

His hands-on approach and deep industry relationships ensure every client receives personalized attention and exceptional results. From initial concept to final walkthrough, Shahbazz is involved in every detail.

Under his leadership, Onward Design Build has completed over 50 projects totaling more than $25 million in development—each one a testament to his commitment to excellence.
